  * [황소자리](%ED%99%A9%EC%86%8C%EC%9E%90%EB%A6%AC.md) : [윤회](%EC%9C%A4%ED%9A%8C.md)왕 **[카오스](%EC%B9%B4%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%28%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%B4%EB%84%90%20%ED%8C%90%ED%83%80%EC%A7%80%29.md)** (輪廻王カオス)
