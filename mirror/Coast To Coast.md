**Coast To Coast**는 [아일랜드](%EC%95%84%EC%9D%BC%EB%9E%9C%EB%93%9C.md)의 [보이밴드](%EB%B3%B4%EC%9D%B4%EB%B0%B4%EB%93%9C.md)인[웨스트라이프](%EC%9B%A8%EC%8A%A4%ED%8A%B8%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%ED%94%84.md)의 두번째 정규 앨범이다.  
이 앨범에서 나온 싱글인 [Against All Odds](Against%20All%20Odds.md), [MyLove](My%20Love.md), [What Makes A Man](What%20Makes%20A%20Man.md), [ILay My Love On You](I%20Lay%20My%20Love%20On%20You.md), [When You'reLooking Like That](When%20You%27re%20%20Looking%20Like%20That.md), [UptownGirl](Uptown%20Girl.md)들 중 [Against All Odds](Against%20All%20Odds.md),
[My Love](My%20Love.md), [uptown Girl](uptown%20Girl.md)이 UK 싱글 차트 1위를
당성했다.
