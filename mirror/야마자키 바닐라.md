## 1. 개요 ¶

山[﨑](%EF%A8%91.md)[バニラ](%EB%B0%94%EB%8B%90%EB%9D%BC/%EB%8F%99%EC%9D%8C%EC%9D%B4%EC%9D%98%EC%96%B4.md)

[성우](%EC%84%B1%EC%9A%B0.md), [변사](%EB%B3%80%EC%82%AC.md),
[탤런트](%ED%83%A4%EB%9F%B0%ED%8A%B8.md). 본명은
[야마자키](%EC%95%BC%EB%A7%88%EC%9E%90%ED%82%A4.md)
[마사미](%EB%A7%88%EC%82%AC%EB%AF%B8.md). [1978년](1978%EB%85%84.md) [1월15일](1%EC%9B%94%2015%EC%9D%BC.md)생.
